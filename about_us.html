Our History


We has been Roseburg's premier plant nursery for over 40 years. In 1977 the Lander family purchased the nursery. With a well-established reputation for quality and knowledgeable service, the store retained the name of Gardening Workshop. Originally the nurseryÂ was known as Sunning Garden & Pets, but over the years the focus of Gardening Workshop has shifted from pets to a full garden center including pond supplies and yard & home decor.


Gardening Workshop Today


Since its foundation, Gardening Workshop has remained a family owned and operated business. If you visit Gardening Workshop today you will still see the owner stocking azaleas, fertilizing baskets, or answering bedding plant questions. The Gardening Workshop team consists of the most knowledgeable gardeners. They are always ready to answer any questions you might have. Mission of Gardening Workshop Gardening Workshop is dedicated to offering you the highest quality products with great service to ensure your gardening success.
